What is Just Bball Stuff

It's really just a website that contains a lot of cool basketball stuff.

What Has Been Done So Far?

The following subsections have been completed:

Defensive Playmaking For every regualr season since 2017-18, get the steal, block, deflections, stocks (steals + blocks), and deals (deflections + steals) for every player. Stats come in the form Total, Per Game, and Per 36 Minutes. Percentile Ranks are kept for players who played at least 500 minutes (when looking at per game and per 36 minutes). All stats are explained more closely in the Glossary section.
Defensive Synergy Get all the defensive playtype data from synergy that has taken place since 2017-18. Playtypes include isolations, pick n roll ball handlers, pick n roll screeners, spot-ups, post-ups, hand-offs, and off-screens. Stats include possessions, points per possessions, frequency of play-type, and percentile of points per possessions.
Matchups For every regualr season since 2017-18, get the matchup data (ie offensive player versus defensive player data) for every player. Also get aggregated data that measures matchup difficulty, matchup versatility, and average matchup size. All stats are explained more closely in the Glossary section.
Rim Protection For every regualr season since 2017-18, get data for shots contested within 6 feet of the rim for all players that were designated Centers or Forward/Centers. Stats come in the form Total, Per Game, and Per 36 Minutes. All stats are explained more closely in the Glossary section.
