Just BBall Stuff

A fun website filled with basketball-related stuff.

Get all the useful, fun stats from stats.nba.com (with enhancements here and there) broken down into easy-to-read subsections. Currently, I am at Matchups, Defensive Playmaking, and Rim Protection but I am still looking to add more sections. All stats start from 2017-18 (the season that Matchup data became available).

This entire project is open-sourced. All code for the website and source data will be publically available on Github repos. If you have any questions, suggestions or potential areas of improvements, feel free to DM me at @schuler76 on Twitter or even feel free to contribute to the codebase/project. And also please feel free to share this project to anyone that can and will benefit from this.

Defensive Playmaking

For every player, get steals, blocks, deflections, stocks (steals + blocks), and deals (deflections + steals) since 2017-18. Stats come in Total, Per Game, and Per 36 Minutes, alonged with rank percentiles for players who played at least 500 minutes.

Matchups

Get all the basic matchup data that has taken place since 2017-18 along with some aggregated stats to track matchup difficulty, versatility against different sized players, average height/weight played against and more to come.

Rim Protection

For every player that has been designated a C per stats.nba.com since 2017-18, get defended FG% along with points saved versus expected. Stats come in Total, Per Game, and Per 36 Minutes, alonged with rank percentiles for players who played at least 500 minutes.
