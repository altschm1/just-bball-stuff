Glossary

Stats are provided in:

  • Season Totals
  • Per Games
  • Per 36 Minutes
PLAYER Name of the Player
TEAM Acronym of Team of the Player
TEAM is whatever team the player finished the season with (according to https://www.nba.com/stats/players/bio/ at least).
AGE Age of the Player at the Start of that Season
HEIGHT Height of the Player in feet and inches
WEIGHT Weight of the Player in pounds
SEASON Regular Season
GP the Number of Games Played in that Season
MP the Number of Minutes Played in that Season
DREB the Number of Defensive Rebounds in that Season
DEF BOX OUT The number of times a player made physical contact with an opponent who was actively pursuing a rebound, showed visible progress or strong effort in disadvantaging the opponent, and successfully prevented that opponent from securing the rebound
DREB RANK the Percentile Rank of Defensive Rebounds for Players that Played at least 500 minutes
DEF BOX OUT RANK the Percentile Rank of Defensive Box Outs for Players that Played at least 500 minutes
