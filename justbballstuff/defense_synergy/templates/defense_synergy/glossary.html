Glossary

Please Note: Defensive Synergy stats are better equipped to describe role and what type of scoring possessions players most frequently defend. There is also a lot of noise to the stats used here. Pick n Roll ball handler and screener point per possession stats can be influenced by the type of defensive coverage (drop vs a more aggressive scheme). Spot-up can be influenced by how aggressive the help defense is. Plus there is also just a lot of noise in these stats due to the fact that these stats don't capture matchup difficulty or deterence. These stats are also vulnerable to the small sample/make-or-miss nature of the league. So use these stats with care and caution.

Stats are provided in:

  • Season Totals
  • Per Games
  • Per 36 Minutes
  • Summary
