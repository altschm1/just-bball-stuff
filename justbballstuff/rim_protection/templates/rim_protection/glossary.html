Glossary

Please Note: First, these stats are only recorded for centers/bigs because these specific stats are really only relevant for centers/bigs (unless future research proves otherwise). That said, rim protection from guards/wings is still an important concept, it just comes in other/much-harder-to-quantify forms: good rotational defense from good stunts to deter drives, tagging rollers, sinking in to cover unguarded lob threats, and good on-ball defense like containing drives one-on-one or being smart in how you navigate on ball and off ball screens. Second, these stats do not encompass all of rim protection as a whole even for bigs. These stats do no quantify the ability to deter shots at the rim which is an extremely important concept of rim protection as a whole. These stats do no quantify how well you defend shots at the rim without fouling which is a really important concept of rim protection as a whole. That said, these stats are a good start for quanitfying rim protection from bigs, just don't let it be the finish line in the discourse.

Stats are provided in:

  • Season Totals
  • Per Games
  • Per 36 Minutes
PLAYER Name of the Player
TEAM Acronym of Team of the Player
TEAM is whatever team the player finished the season with (according to https://www.nba.com/stats/players/bio/ at least).
AGE Age of the Player at the Start of that Season
HEIGHT Height of the Player in feet and inches
WEIGHT Weight of the Player in pounds
SEASON Regular Season
GP the Number of Games Played in that Season
MP the Number of Minutes Played in that Season
FREQ% of all Field Goal Attempts contested, how many of them were within 6 feet of the rim
DFGM Of All Field Goal Attempts contested within 6 feet of the rim, how field goal attempts were made
DFGA How Many Field Goal Attempts Contested With in 6 feet of the Rim
DFG% the FG% of shots contested within 6 feet of the rim
DFG% SCORE the DFG% percentile among players who played at least 500 minutes
Diff% the difference in actual FG% of shots conested at the rim and predicted FG% of shots contested within 6 feet of the rim
Diff% SCORE the Diff% percentile among players who played at least 500 minutes
